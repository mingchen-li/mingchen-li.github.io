Mingchen Li (李明辰)  

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science and Technology at East China University of Science and Technology (expected to graduate in June 2025), under the supervision of Professor Huiqun Yu and Professor Guisheng Fan. I received my Bachelor of Science degree in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics in 2020 from East China University of Science and Technology. My research interests focus on deep-learning-based protein design and software engineering.
我是华东理工大学计算机科学与技术专业博士生(预期2025年6月毕业), 导师为虞慧群教授。 我2020年本科毕业于华东理工大学数学与应用数学专业。 我的研究兴趣包括基于深度学习的蛋白质设计，同时我还对软件工程有研究经验。
